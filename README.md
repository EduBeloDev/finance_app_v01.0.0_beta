An interactive web application designed for couples to manage their monthly budget, visualize cash flow, and plan long-term financial goals. This app is built to be lightweight, privacy-focused, and installable as a mobile application (PWA).

## üöÄ Key Features

* **Interactive Timeline:** A daily view of your finances with interactive cards for every day of the month.
* **Dynamic Evolution Chart:** Visual canvas chart to monitor your balance, income, and expenses at a glance.
* **Income Management:** Support for multiple pay dates per person via an integrated pop-up calendar.
* **Real-time Cash Flow:** Automatic calculation of "Daily Spending Limits" and "End of Month" projections.
* **Loan & Goal Simulator:** Professional-grade calculator for long-term goals and loans using compound interest (Price Table).
* **Mobile Ready (PWA):** Can be installed on Android and iOS devices for a native app experience.

## üì± How to Install on Your Mobile

Since this is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, you don't need an App Store:

### **On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the app link in Chrome.
2. Tap the **three dots** in the top right corner.
3. Select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.

### **On iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the app link in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with an upward arrow).
3. Scroll down and select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
